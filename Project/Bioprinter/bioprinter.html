Cosmyx Bioprinter

Project Overview

The project consists of customizing a Cosmyx printer into a bioprinter. Drawing inspiration from numerous open source projects and research papers, the goal is to transform an accessible 3D printer into a bioprinter. Indeed, bioprinters on the market are very expensive, even entry-level models. Current applications are mainly medical, but through this project, we hope to contribute to innovation in the printing of living structures. The Cosmyx also has the advantage of having one of the highest printing speeds among 3D printers on the market, so we used it to build an open-source bioprinter with one of the highest printing speeds.

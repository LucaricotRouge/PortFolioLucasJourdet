Paper Overview

This paper follows on from the innovation project on mycelium oven and the observation that there are no papers dealing with the evolution of the mechanical and thermal properties of mycelium composites over repeated high-temperature thermal cycles. The objective of this paper is therefore to characterize the thermo-mechanical properties of mycelium composites that have undergone repeated thermal cycles between 100 and 200Â°C. Knowledge of these characteristics would make it possible to more accurately assess the strength and viability of a speculative object such as a mycelium oven for everyday use.

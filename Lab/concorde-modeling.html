Concorde Modeling

Project Overview

3D modeling and simulation of the iconic Concorde supersonic aircraft combining aeronautical design with digital fabrication.

Design & Engineering

Detailed recreation of the Concorde with accurate aerodynamic properties and structural analysis.

Key Components

  • Aerodynamic modeling
  • Structural analysis
  • 3D visualization
  • Performance simulation

Applications

This project demonstrates advanced modeling techniques in aerospace engineering and provides insights into supersonic aircraft design.

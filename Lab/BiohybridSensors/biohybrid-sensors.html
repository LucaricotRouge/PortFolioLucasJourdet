Biohybrid Sensors

Project Overview

Research and development of advanced biohybrid sensor systems that integrate biological and technological components.

Research Focus

The project explores the intersection of biotechnology and material science to create innovative sensing solutions.

Key Achievements

  • Novel sensor design architecture
  • Biocompatible material selection
  • Real-time data processing
  • Clinical applications potential

Impact & Applications

This research opens new possibilities in medical diagnostics and environmental monitoring.
